Full Description

Our Grilled Chicken Salad is prepared using premium grilled chicken breast, freshly picked lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, sweet corn, red onion, black olives, parmesan cheese, and creamy avocado. It is tossed with our signature homemade lemon-herb dressing to deliver a refreshing and healthy flavor.

This dish is perfect for lunch or dinner and provides a balanced combination of lean protein, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. Every ingredient is carefully selected to ensure freshness and exceptional taste in every bite.
